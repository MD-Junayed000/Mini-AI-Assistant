Wireless Mouse (SKU-001)
========================

The Wireless Mouse is an ergonomic Bluetooth mouse with a silent-click sensor.
Battery life is approximately 18 months on a single AA cell. The mouse pairs
via Bluetooth 5.0 and supports both Windows and macOS without additional
drivers. Pairing: hold the small button on the base for three seconds, then
select the device from the host's Bluetooth menu.

USB-C Hub (SKU-002)
==================

A 7-port USB-C hub providing HDMI, two USB-A 3.0, SD, microSD, 100 W PD
passthrough, and gigabit Ethernet. The hub draws bus power and is rated for
5 Gbps on the USB-A ports. Compatible with macOS, Windows, and Linux.

Mechanical Keyboard (SKU-007)
============================

Tactile mechanical keyboard with hot-swappable switches, RGB backlight, and
USB-C detachable cable. The keyboard supports n-key rollover over USB and is
programmable through an open firmware tool.

Laptop Stand (SKU-014)
======================

Aluminum laptop stand with six height adjustments. Supports laptops up to 17
inches and 4.5 kg. The stand folds flat for travel and ships with a felt
sleeve.
